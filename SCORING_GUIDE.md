###Things to look for while scoring:

#####The idea:
- Does the idea make sense?
- How unique is the idea?

#####The MVP:
- Does it make sense when you use it?
- Does it look nice?
- Does it impress you?
- Would it make sense to the average user?

#####Implementation:
- Did they use the Ethereum blockchain and IPFS?
